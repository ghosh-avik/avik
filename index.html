I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in UC San Diego ECE working with Professors Yuanyuan Shi and Sujit Dey. I got my PhD from the MAE department, also at UC San Diego, being jointly advised by Professors Jan Kleissl and Sonia Martínez, in 2025. I was as a PhD intern at the Pacific Northwest National Laboratory in the summer of 2023, working with Dr. Veronica Adetola. Prior to joining UC San Diego, I completed his Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur in 2019, where I worked with Prof. Aritra Ganguly. I also spent the summer of 2018 at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur working with Prof. Jishnu Bhattacharya.

I can be reached at av[lastname]AT[ucsd]DOT[edu]. My office is in Franklin Antonio Hall, Collaboratory 2300. I am always interested in working with undergraduate/graduate students. See Research statement, Recent news and Publications to know about my research.
